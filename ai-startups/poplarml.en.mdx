**PoplarML** is a powerful AI tool designed to simplify the deployment of machine learning (ML) models in production environments. Its intuitive command-line interface (CLI) tool makes it accessible to both beginners and experienced data scientists, streamlining the deployment process and ensuring efficient and high-performance inference on a fleet of GPUs.

### Standout Features

One of the standout features of PoplarML is its ability to enable real-time inference through a REST API. This means that you can easily invoke your deployed models to make predictions on new data in real-time, making it immensely useful for applications that require immediate responses, such as fraud detection, chatbots, or recommendation systems.

Another key advantage of PoplarML is its scalability. By deploying models to a fleet of GPUs, you can leverage their parallel processing capabilities to achieve faster and more efficient inference. This is especially important when dealing with large datasets or complex models that require significant computational resources.
